Dewin Mawr Postmortem

Recently I just finished participating in the GMTK Game Jam with three other incredible game devs. Whenever I finish a game like this I like to look back and see what I've learnt from the project. Especially this time, I have learnt quite a sizable amount.

I like working in a team, actually

Most of my previous attempts at working in a team were largely unsuccesful for one reason or another. That means until now, I had no idea what it was like to see a project to the (or "a") finish line.

And I have to say, I like it! A lot, actually. I'd love to some more work in a team. My main problems with solo game dev (lack of motivation, emotional fluctuations) kind of disappeared with the silent pressure of a group setting. Those things still happened, but the fact that other people working on it brought me back to reality pretty quick.
