Hey I've got a few projects that I touch back on sometimes

Dewin Mawr

Play it here!

Dewin Mawr is a polished puzzle platformer. I made it with 3 other people in 4 days for the GMTK 2024 game jam. Out of 7600 particpants, we placed 325th overall and 101st in the enjoyment category. The game seems to be very popular! You play as a wizard who can change size with the help of magical crystals. The bigger you are the higher you can jump; but you can't fit into small spaces.

HIVE

Check it out!

HIVE is an experiment in engine-less game development (spoiler alert: engine-less dev is very very hard). It's a tile based puzzler where you control an entire hive of bees at once as they go to flowers to prepare for winter. Still under development; I hope to finish it; sometime this year. Is it the most accurate to real-life flesh and blood bees? Probably not. It is what it is.
